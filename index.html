About The Band

Built for a packed room and a loud encore.

The Bayhounds blend country storytelling, southern grit, and rock-and-roll punch into a show that hits hard from the first song to the last call. Whether it’s a festival stage, a rowdy bar crowd, a wedding dance floor, or a private event, the goal is the same: keep the room alive.
We bring the kind of set that feels wild, tight, and memorable — the soundtrack for late nights, raised glasses, and boots on the floor.
Perfect For
