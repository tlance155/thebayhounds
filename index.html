About the Band

Country rock that feels made for a packed room.

The Bayhounds blend country storytelling, rock attitude, and crowd-favorite energy into a set that keeps people singing, dancing, and asking when you’re coming back. Whether it’s a downtown venue, outdoor festival, corporate event, private party, or wedding, they bring a polished live performance with a rugged edge.

Looking for live music that fits your crowd and keeps the night moving? The Bayhounds can tailor the set for high-energy bar nights, laid-back acoustic-style moments, or full-band feature shows.
